Line-Following Robot — Design & Control

Published Dec 11, 2024 · By Shubham Kulkarni

Overview

A practical robotics project using an Arduino Mega and IR sensors to track and follow lines. We focus on sensor placement, debouncing, PID-like control and robust tuning for different surfaces.

Hardware & Sensors

  • Arduino Mega / Uno
  • IR reflectance sensors or QTR modules
  • Motor driver with encoder feedback for better control

Control Strategy

Simple proportional control on the error signal between left and right sensors works well. For better performance use PID and encoders, and perform surface-specific calibration.
